Empowering Communities, Cultivating Prosperity

Rhino-MAC Agro Bees Ltd was founded by graduates from Makerere University's College of Veterinary Medicine to address unemployment and the need for better extension services in Northern Uganda. Our mission is to enhance rural livelihoods through consultancy, quality products, and services, including artificial insemination, waste management, insect farming, and poultry inputs.
