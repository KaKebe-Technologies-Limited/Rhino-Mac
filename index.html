Services

Organic Farm Services

Contact Us

Insect farming

Bees: We support beekeeping initiatives, providing expertise in bee farming to boost honey production and pollination. Our services include hive management, pest control, and honey extraction. Black Soldier Fly: We also promote black soldier fly farming, which is a sustainable method for producing protein-rich feed for livestock and recycling organic waste.

Artificial insermination

We provide advanced artificial insemination services to improve livestock genetics. This technique helps farmers enhance the quality of their herds by using superior breeds, which can lead to increased productivity and better health in their animals.

Poultry Inputs

We supply essential commercial poultry inputs such as feed, vitamins, and supplements to ensure healthy growth and high productivity in poultry farming. Concentrates: Our concentrates are specially formulated to enhance the nutritional value of poultry feed, improving the overall health and performance of poultry.

Day-old chicks

We offer day-old chicks for poultry farming, providing farmers with healthy and high-quality chicks that are ideal for starting or expanding their poultry operations. Our service includes support on chick care and management to ensure optimal growth and productivity.

Waste management

Our waste management services focus on efficient disposal and recycling of agricultural and organic waste. We offer solutions that help farmers manage waste sustainably, reducing environmental impact and promoting soil health.
